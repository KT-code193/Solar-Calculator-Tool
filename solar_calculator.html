Solar Recycling Profitability Calculator

How to Find This Information

  • Number of Panels: Check your installation documents or count panels manually.
  • Panel Weight: Usually listed in the manufacturer's datasheet (typically 18–25 kg).
  • Recycling & Landfill Costs: Contact local recycling centers or waste management companies for quotes.
  • Transport Cost: Estimate fuel or shipping costs per km per panel (or ask recyclers for an estimate).
  • Distance to Site: Use Google Maps to measure distance from your location to the recycler or landfill.
  • Subsidies or Fines: Refer to government or environmental agency policies for any financial incentives or penalties.
  • Material Recovery Revenue: Ask recyclers what materials are recovered (silver, silicon, etc.) and how much they pay per kg.

If you're unsure about any value, use average estimates or consult with a solar recycler to get more accurate data.
